Keema Noodles recipe

Ingrediants

  • 1 pack spaghetti
  • 500 gm chicken mince
  • thinly sliced onion
  • 1/2 cup carrots, shreeded
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 cup boiled peas
  • 2tbs olive oil
  • 1tbs soy sauce
  • 1tb black pepper
  • red pepper

Cooking process


  1. Step 1:Boil the Spaghetti in a large sauce pan. Follow directions on box for cooking. .


  2. Step 2: When spaghetti is cooked, rinse with cold water. Set aside.


  3. Step 3: Put oil in a large fry pan and cook the chicken mince. Cook on medium to high heat, till the meat is well done.


  4. Step 4: Now add the carrots, olives and green peas. Cook for a few minutes.


  5. Step 5: Add the rest of the ingredients, and stir fry.


  6. Step 6: Keep stirring for a few minutes, now add the spaghetti and mix well.


  7. Step 7: When the spaghetti and vegetables are mixed well, add the vinegar and soy sauce. Cook for another 2 minutes.


  8. Step 8: Dish it out. Keema Noodles is ready to serve. Serve immediately.
